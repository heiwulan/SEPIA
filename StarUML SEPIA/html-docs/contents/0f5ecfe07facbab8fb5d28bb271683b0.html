Description

Abstract class that defines general properties for flow relations between Petri net places and transitions.<br> Flow relations can lead from transitions to places or the other way round (direction).<br> The direction of the flow relation is defined with the help of the {@link #directionPT}-property.<br> <br> Flow relations have a unique name which is determined by concatenation of place and transition names<br> and the direction property (they have also unique names). @author Thomas Stocker @param <P> The type of Petri net places. @param <T> The type of Petri net transitions.
